Volunteer to participate in vaccine trials

SingHealth is looking for adult volunteers to participate in the next trial for a vaccine candidate that is co-developed by Duke-NUS Medical School and American pharmaceutical company Arcturus Therapeutics. This can help Singapore to manufacture their local vaccine to eradicate Covid-19.
