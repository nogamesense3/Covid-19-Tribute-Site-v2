Ms Quek's story


Ms Quek was part of the CGH Mobile Rapid Triage team and she was responsible for managing the team's operations and conducted medical tests such as swab tests which will be critical to mitigate and contain the Covid-19 pandemic by identifying the infected individuals.

She is a team player and is detail oriented and she is also proud to serve the nation and protect people from invisible enemies such as Covid-19.

Ms Rasif's story

Ms Jade Rasif is a famous local DJ. Ever since the Coronavirus pandemic started, she has been serving in the healthcare sector by volunteering with the Singapore Healthcare Corps , subsequently underwent training and get deployed to places such as worker dormitories and hotels.

She is also selfless as she sacrificed all sources of income such as sponsorships or any media apperances. She has a strong passion to serve the nation as she seeks to pursue a nursing degree and become a nurse in the future.

Ms Nerissa's story

At the National Cancer Centre Singapore’s (NCCS) Ambulatory Treatment Unit (ATU), Ms Nerissa cares for patients that are suffering from cancer and is more vulnerable to Covid-19. She feels a great sense of fulfilment in serving her patients and adopt a positive attitude to motivate her colleagues despite the unprecedented challenges.

Ms Fairman's story

Ms Fairman is a respiratory therapist and she serves patients with a range of breathing problems such as asthma to heart attacks. She is brave as she she is exposed to Covid-19 infected patients on a daily basis and she is also devoted to her family by checking with her older son and daughter and spending more time with her husband and her younger son.

Dr Luigi Cavanna's story

Dr Luigi is the head of an oncology ward and ever since the Covid-19 pandemic started, he saw a surge in cases that could have been treated before it was severe. Due to the remoteness of most homes in villages from the hospital, the symptoms of infected patients could worsen without treatment since medical treatment is not readily accessible. Dr Luigi and his team have been selfless as they have went around Piacenza to treat infected patients with medicine a device that monitors the levels of oxygen in the blood.

Dr. Stanley M. Berry's story

Dr. Stanley is an obstetrician who specializes in high-risk pregnancy. He is selfless as he was planning to retire and finish his novel in the future but despite discouragements from his friends and the fear of contracting the Covid-19 virus, he decided to volunteer to join critical care in Detroit to contribute by treating infected patients.
